About Buildana

We don’t just build structures. We build trust, relationships, and peace of mind.

Buildana was founded with a clear purpose: to bring trust, transparency, and quality back into the building experience. We're a proud, family-owned construction company based in Sydney, and we understand that building is about more than just materials — it’s about people.

The name “Buildana” reflects the core of who we are. Derived from the Arabic word Amanah (أمانة), it means trust, honesty, and integrity — values we uphold in every project, big or small.

Whether you’re creating a new home for your family, upgrading your investment property, or undertaking a complex duplex or townhouse project, our team treats every build as if it were our own. That’s what makes us different.

At Buildana, we’ve intentionally built a reputation not just on the quality of our construction — but on the strength of our relationships. We keep our word, communicate clearly, and deliver what we promise. No excuses. No hidden surprises. Just results.

Our Services

From new builds to renovations, we’re equipped to handle your project with care, clarity, and craftsmanship.

No two building projects are the same — and that’s exactly why our services are designed to be flexible, responsive, and tailored to what you need.

  • Custom Homes: Bespoke new builds designed around your family, lifestyle, and land.
  • Granny Flats: Self-contained dwellings that add value, flexibility, and comfort.
  • Renovations & Extensions: Thoughtful upgrades to enhance the way you live — without moving.
  • Knockdown Rebuilds: Clear the old and rebuild with a home that’s truly your own.
  • Dual Occupancies & Duplexes: Smart property solutions for families or investment growth.
  • Townhouses & Multi-Unit Projects: Medium-density builds with high-end finishes.
  • Project Management: End-to-end oversight for a smooth and supported building journey.
